How to Play:

First, begin by loging in. Once you have logged in, you are all set to play! Either you or your partner will begin asking a question, and the other will answer the question. If you are asking a question, type any two numbers. Then ask your partner to multiply it. If your partner gets the right answer, they get a point. Continue playing the game to see who wins!
